Tic-Tac-Toe

◆  JAVAFX  ·  MVC  ·  AI  ·  CERI AVIGNON  ◆

✦

This project was completed during my third year of university with a teammate, Samy Seghir. The application is a Tic-Tac-Toe game built in JavaFX, allowing players to compete in local multiplayer or against an AI. The AI's difficulty can be adjusted on the model creation page. A settings page also lets players switch to fullscreen mode and adjust the sound volume.

For my part, I was mainly responsible for the game logic and AI training, while my teammate handled the graphical interface and the application's settings.

Main features:

  • Developed in JavaFX with MVC architecture
  • Local multiplayer mode
  • Option to play against an AI
  • Manually adjustable AI difficulty
  • Win and loss animations
  • Sound design — background music & sound effects
