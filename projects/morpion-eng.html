Tic-Tac-Toe

This project was completed during my third year of university with a teammate. The application is a Tic-Tac-Toe game built in JavaFX, allowing players to compete in local multiplayer or against an AI. The AI's difficulty can be adjusted on the AI model creation page. Additionally, a settings page allows players to switch to fullscreen mode and adjust the sound volume.

For my part, I was mainly responsible for the game logic and AI training, while my teammate handled the graphical interface and the application's settings.

Main features:

  • Developed in JavaFX
  • MVC Architecture
  • Local multiplayer
  • Option to play against an AI
  • Manually adjustable AI difficulty
  • Win and loss animations
  • Sound design (background music & sound effects)
