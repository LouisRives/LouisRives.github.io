Hyper Pulsar

Immerse yourself in a fast-paced, die and retry 2D action game where precise movement, well-timed parries, and strategic combat define every encounter. As a skilled fighter, you’ll navigate dynamic environments, counter enemy attacks, and master the art of dodging and reflecting projectiles. With intense AI-driven enemies, fluid animations, and a deep combat system, every battle is a test of skill and reflexes.

During my gap year, I am dedicating myself to creating an indie game that I am passionate about. Inspired by masterpieces like Hotline Miami and Katana Zero, this project takes place in a brutal, stylized neo-noir universe where the action is both intense and thoughtful.

The player takes on the role of an intergalactic bounty hunter, tracking targets across different planets. Each one is a unique level, with its own theme, atmosphere, and gameplay mechanics. The goal? Find, fight, and eliminate space criminals. Right now, I’m working on the Casino planet. Behind its flashy neon lights hides a world of betrayals, high-stakes gambling, and intense shootouts. I strive to create immersive environments where strategy and reflexes are your best allies.
