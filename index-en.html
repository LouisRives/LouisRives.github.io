My Journey

✦

Hey, welcome to my portfolio!

I'm Louis, a French-Finnish developer currently doing my Master's in Game Technology at BUAS in Breda. Before that I did my Bachelor's in Computer Science in Avignon, with an Erasmus semester in Brno thrown in for good measure. Programming is what I know best, and making games is what I actually want to do with it.

I've been obsessed with video games since I was a kid — the kind of obsession that eventually turns into "okay I need to make these things myself". I've got 150h+ in GameMaker across personal projects, mainly Hyper Pulsar, my hardcore top-down shooter inspired by Hotline Miami and Katana Zero. I'm also a huge souls-like fan — Dark Souls, Elden Ring, the whole deal — which is actually what my Master's thesis is about. Right now I'm also picking up Blender and Unreal Engine 5 to round things out.

I care a lot about game feel — the kind of stuff players notice without knowing why. If you're working on something in that space and need someone who can code and genuinely loves games, feel free to reach out!
