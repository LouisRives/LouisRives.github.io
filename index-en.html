My Journey

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

Passionate about computer science and video games, I live in the south of France. I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science at CERI Avignon, where I developed strong skills in software development and design. In my third year, I had the chance to study abroad in Brno as part of the Erasmus program, an incredibly enriching experience that allowed me to adapt to an English-speaking environment and broaden my personal and professional horizons.

Since childhood, video games have played a key role in my life. What started as a simple hobby has, over time, become a true passion for game development. I’ve explored this world through various projects, both academic and personal. Currently, during my gap year, I am fully dedicated to a project that is very close to my heart: creating a hardcore top-down shooter game, inspired by titles like Hotline Miami and Katana Zero. I am working with GameMaker, a tool I am now familiar with, allowing me to unleash my creativity.

Additionally, my dual French-Finnish nationality has given me the opportunity to travel from an early age, sparking in me an insatiable curiosity for new horizons. This desire for discovery now drives me to pursue a Master's degree abroad, to continue growing both personally and professionally.

I invite you to explore my projects and follow my journey in the exciting world of game development. Thank you for visiting!
